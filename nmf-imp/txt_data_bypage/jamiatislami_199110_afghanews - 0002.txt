Baghlan Province
The Mujahideen from Jami'at attacked the enemy base in old city of Baghlan on September 20 killing 14 soldiers wounding 21 and destroying one tank. Commander Haqjoo reported. In retaliation, regime planes and artillery bombed the Mujahideen position in the out skirts of the city destroying a number of houses and setting on fire many harvests. Many civilians left their houses and went to the safer areas.
Three Mujahideen were killed and one Mujahid sustained injuries.
Balkh Province
•The Mujahideen attacked the Kabul regime's security posts along the Sheberghan-Mazar road on August 22 destroying many posts and inflicting heavy losses cm the enemy. Commander Mawlawi Mohammad Alam reported. The Mujahideen seized 22 Kalashnikov rifles, one machinegun and some ammunition.
•The Mujahideen of Jami’at canned more than 200 regime personnel including 18 officer in Faizabad sub-District, Blakh District on September 19, Hindukush News Agence reported.
They detstroyed several regime security posts and seized one BM-41 rocket launcher, two tanks, 600 pieces of light and heavy weapons and several Radio sets of R-10 and R-104 types. The report gives no information about the casulaties of the two sides.
Jawzjan Province
The Mujahideen of Jami'at destroyed six enemy security posts, killed 25 militiamen and wounded 30 in Sara-j i-Pul District in three days of fighting j which began on September 6, | Hindukush News Agency reported.
This victory led to the liberation of Kharzar area and capture of 35 guns by die Mujahideen. The militias killed or wounded belonged to the notorious gang of Rashid Dostum.
